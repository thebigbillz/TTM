ABOUT US

We are a young, driven, ambitious and vibrant team of Creatives, Digital Experts, Brand Managers and Strategist committed to telling Client stories and evolving the science of advertising and event management by leveraging international best practices to achieve desired result.

Ours is a culture of newness that sets us and our clientele apart from the rest of the crop in the BTL, OOH, ATL, Digital and Creative spheres.

Think Tank has over time revolutionized the media practice in Nigeria by initiating standards to ensure unprecedented high media compliance by media owners. As an agency, we are focused on developing and providing world class digital media and event management services to leading local and international clients across this sub region.

In 2017, TTMA launched a high-profile event for the benefit of the creative industry in Nigeria called the Creative Nigeria Summit. The sole purpose of which was to bridge the gap between the Nigerian government and the Creative industry thereby benefitting every sector within the creative industry and also creating crucial policies to help in the industries expansion in Nigeria - this event has been in existence since 2017.

At TTMA, our goal is to change the perception of digital media and advertising in Nigeria, and we intend to do this by delivering innovative, creative, and effective ideas for our clients and their brands every time.
